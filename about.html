More About Me

One of my biggest inspirations comes from online creative communities. I’ve grown up watching YouTube creators and artists build entire worlds through humor, storytelling, and digital art. Seeing how these spaces bring people together made me realize how much power media has to connect us. I try to bring that same sense of joy and creativity into my work — whether it’s a video, a drawing, or a small interactive project.

Outside of art, I enjoy visiting cafés with friends, sketching, and exploring the Boston area. These little moments help me recharge and find inspiration for future projects.

Some of My Interests:

  • Video Editing & Motion Design
  • Digital Illustration
  • K-Pop and Japanese City Pop Music
  • Gaming and YouTube Culture
